I work on problems where **data integrity and methodological soundness are non-negotiable** — boundary harmonisation for administrative datasets, disaster risk modelling, and AI-augmented geospatial analysis. My projects tend to start with a research question and end with a deployed system.

### 🗺 [I-ASCAP](https://github.com/Satyamkumar2610/I-ASCAP)
**Indian Agri-Spatial Comparative Analytics Platform**

Research-grade platform for analysing Indian agricultural evolution at district level, **1966–2024**. The central problem: Indian administrative districts have split, merged, and been renamed hundreds of times — making naive longitudinal comparison statistically invalid.

**Key engineering:** A custom `DistrictHarmonizer` class that apportions historical crop data to modern boundaries via topological splits, enabling sound time-series analysis across six decades of administrative discontinuity. Full district lineage tracking included.

`Next.js 14` `FastAPI` `PostGIS` `GeoPandas` `Docker`
